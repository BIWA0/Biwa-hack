I Miss Us

Evenings feel different now…

That quiet time we used to talk… now it just echoes.

You’re out there growing.

And getting out of those stress feelings — I’m really proud of you.

But I miss the ‘every 2-hour’ you…

Those random messages, little updates… they were my warmth.

Now coffee breaks are quieter.

I sip mine alone, in our chat box, reading our past evening-time conversations… pretending we're still talking.

I love you. I’ll wait for you.

It’s just that… it became quieter around me after almost 6 months. You’re the only person I’ve been talking to all this time.

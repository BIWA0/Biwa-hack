I Miss Us

Evenings feel different now...

I sit with my tea, reading our last conversations and pretending we're still talking.

You’re out there growing.

And getting out of the stress you were stuck in. I’m really proud of you.

I miss the ‘every 2-hour’ you…

The one who sent random messages in the middle of study breaks.

Coffee breaks are quieter now…

I sip mine alone, reading our past conversations, pretending we're still mid-chat.

It's just that...

It became quieter around me after almost 6 months of talking only to you.

I love you. I’ll wait for you.

You’re the only person I’ve been talking to all these months.

I Miss Us

Evenings feel so different now...

The quiet moments we used to share... now they just feel empty.

You’re out there, living your own story...

And I’m so happy you’re growing, but sometimes I just wish I was a bigger part of it.

I miss the little ‘us’ we had every two hours...

The random updates, the small talk, the daily check-ins... it’s those moments I miss the most.

Now, coffee breaks feel quieter.

I sip my coffee, and I think about how much I miss hearing from you.

I’ll wait for you, no matter how quiet things get...

You’ve been the one person I’ve talked to for so long, and even though it’s quieter now, I’m still here, quietly loving you.
